Dunces & Dragons

by Bhavya & Nyjayia

Embark on a quirky dungeon quest where chaos meets courage! In Dunces & Dragons, you play as a brave hobbit facing off against mischief-making goblins and slime monsters. Survive waves of enemies, pop magical bubble traps, and answer bizarre riddles to prove your wit and might. Will you outsmart the underworld's oddest creatures?
